YourNews is my first attempt to provide users with news articles according to their interests. News articles are crawled every two hours, indexed, and then provided to users according to their specific needs. The personalization for different user interests are made by keeping track of the news articles they had read and storing the information to user models. For different scopes and topics, multiple user models are constructed and maintained, so that users can switch to different user models according to their needs. The user models are also visible and open, and the users can see what's going on under the hood and even control the contents of them.


Published : Ahn, J., Brusilovsky, P., Grady, J., He, D., and Syn, S. Y. (2007) Open user profiles for adaptive news systems: help or harm? In: Proceedings of the 16th international conference on World Wide Web, WWW '07, Banff, Canada, May 8-12, 2007, ACM Press, pp. 11-20. [PDF]
