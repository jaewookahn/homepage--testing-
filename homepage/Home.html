Hi. I am Jae-wook Ahn, a data scientist at the College of Computing and Informatics, Drexel University.  I worked as an NSF-CRA Computing Innovation Fellow at the Human-Computer Interaction Lab, University of Maryland.

I received my Ph.D. from the School of Information Sciences, University of Pittsburgh and do research on personalized/social information retrieval, human-computer interaction, and (adaptive) social network information analysis/visualization.

Please see the research and publication pages and my Curriculum Vitae for more details.
